Station Record

Foundation registry / 3311

ARGINE was established in 3311 by a loose group of independent commanders, long-range pilots and logistics operators who required a stable orbital stop outside the usual central traffic lanes.

The original intent was simple: provide a fixed point for docking, regrouping and scheduled transfers without the overhead of a major faction terminal.

From that need, ARGINE Outpost was deployed in orbit around the primary star of Trianguli Sector JN-S a4-0, becoming a recognizable waypoint for transit crews, explorers and carrier passengers.
